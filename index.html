What We Do

Ethic Talent recognizes the responsibility people feel to support themselves and their family, and the responsibility for us at Ethic Talent as a social enterprise to help broker opportunities for members of our community looking for work. We are devoted to the highest standards of ethics and integrity. We work with companies who want to be ethical in their hiring practices and with their impact on their community. We have proven reputation for leadership and community engagement, communication, outreach, and building bridges. Our philosophy is to also have a transformative impact within Waterloo Region and beyond. Waterloo Region is a premier destination because of its exceptional social mobility incubator with our thriving tech hub and robust manufacturing sector. We will leverage our place within the Region and help individuals find their path towards social mobility and economic prosperity.

About Us

Ethic Talent Corp. Staffing Solutions is a training, coaching consulting, and employment agency offering an end-to-end solution to the Canadian experience gap. The agency provides in three interrelated categories:

  1. Career development training and coaching
  2. Employment and placement agency
  3. Equity, diversity and inclusion training and consulting
